# Wishees Facebook Health Content Plan - 2026-06-23

Goal: make `https://www.facebook.com/wisheescom/` feel like an active Wishees page for Vietnamese-first gift moments, not an abandoned/legacy page.

Primary audience: Vietnamese speakers first; English support only when it helps.

Content rule: post warm, realistic gift moments. Do not over-explain the product. Make each post answer one normal gift problem.

## Health Definition

The page is healthy when:

1. The first visible posts clearly look like Wishees, not old wedding-card content.
2. A new visitor immediately understands: Wishees helps people create and share simple wishlists.
3. The page has repeated Vietnamese-first gift situations: birthday, baby shower, family gifts, group gifts, wedding/registry supplement.
4. The copy feels human and useful, not like a SaaS ad.
5. There is at least one pinned/intro post and several recent posts before more paid outreach or creator pitching.

## Weekly Cadence

Post 3 to 4 times per week for the first two weeks:

- Day 1: Post A pinned intro/birthday.
- Day 2 or 3: Post B baby shower.
- Day 4 or 5: Post C group gift.
- Day 6 or 7: Post D creator/favorite things.
- Week 2: repeat occasion mix with wedding, family gifts, class gifts, and another birthday angle.
- Community gift list can enter after at least three normal gift posts are visible.

Do not post more than once per day until the page looks stable and reactions are measurable.

## Measurement

After each post, record:

- Live Facebook URL.
- Post type: Reel, photo, or link/photo post.
- First visible views/reactions/comments/shares.
- Whether Vietnamese comments/questions appear.
- Whether Wishees statistics move: shares created, link opened, connections, gift reservations.

Do not judge a post only by views. A healthy page needs recognizable identity and repeated useful use cases before conversion improves.
