## Audience Rule

Primary audience:

- Vietnamese speakers first.
- Gift planners, parents, couples, family organizers, and friends buying gifts.

Secondary audience:

- English-speaking users who understand simple gift/wishlist language.

Copy pattern:

1. Vietnamese hook first.
2. English supporting sentence second only when useful.
3. Keep copy short and practical.
4. Focus on real gift moments, not product features.
