# Wishees Outreach Follow-Up Template - 2026-06-22

Use only after an approved first message was sent.

Do not follow up if:

- the first message bounced,
- the form rejected the message as promotional/spam,
- the creator replied negatively,
- the sender inbox reputation looks risky.

## Timing

Send one follow-up 4-7 days after first contact.

Do not send more than one follow-up in this first experiment.

## Subject

Use the original subject with `Re:` if sending by email.

## After Sending

Update `wishees-us-pinterest-leads-2026-06-22.csv`:

1. Set `Date of Follow-up`.
