## Day 7 - Decision Review

Measure:

1. Outreach sent.
2. Replies.
3. Positive replies.
4. Demos requested.
5. Affiliate program joined/applied.
6. Affiliate tags saved.
7. Published social URLs.
8. Objections or confusion.

Decision:

- Continue same channel if at least one creator replies positively, asks for a demo, joins/applies, or saves tags.
- Adjust copy if creators misunderstand who pays commission, whether Wishees replaces existing tools, or whether their tags stay theirs.
- Pause sending if contact forms reject messages as promotional/spam or sender reputation looks risky.
