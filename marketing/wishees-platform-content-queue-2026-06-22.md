## Profile Baseline

Use the same plain positioning across all three accounts:

> Wishees helps people create simple shareable wishlists for birthdays, weddings, baby showers, holidays, family gifts, and creator audiences.

Link target:

> https://www.wishees.com/?lang=en

Fallback if only one link can be used:

> https://www.wishees.com/affiliate/program?lang=en

Do not say Wishees pays creators. Say creator tags stay theirs and stores/networks pay under their normal rules.

Public link rule: use the English homepage for broad gift-moment content and the English affiliate-program page for creator/affiliate content. The shared demo wish URLs are useful as examples for interested creators, but they ask public visitors to connect/register before showing the full wishlist.

## Content Enrichment Rule

While outreach waits for replies, keep the channels useful and credible. The content should make a stranger understand Wishees in one glance:

- Use concrete gift moments, not abstract product claims.
- Show why a wishlist helps the giver, not only the list owner.
- Repeat the positioning: Wishees works alongside Amazon, registries, LTK, ShopMy, and creator storefronts.
- Do not say creators must switch tools.
- Do not publish more TikTok until the duplicated hashtag issue is fixed or avoided by using captions with no repeated hashtag block.
