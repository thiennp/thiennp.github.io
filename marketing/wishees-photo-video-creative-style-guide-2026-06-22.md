## Why This Style

Pinterest, TikTok, and Shorts should make people feel the gift moment first. The old graphic-style assets are clear, but they feel more like product ads. Realistic photo-led assets should be better for discovery because they match how users search and save ideas: birthday cakes, baby shower tables, wrapped gifts, wedding gift tables, classroom gifts, family moments.

## Visual Rules

1. Use one concrete gift moment per asset.
2. Use realistic photography or realistic AI-photo output.
3. Keep the scene bright and emotionally clear.
4. Leave clean space for text, usually top third or lower third.
5. Use overlay text of 10 words or fewer.
6. Use high contrast text that is readable on mobile.
7. Keep `Wishees.com` small and visible.
8. Avoid clutter, fake testimonials, fake app screens, or too many product claims.
9. Avoid generic tech/SaaS visuals.
10. Avoid dark, blurry, stocky, or purely atmospheric images.

## Text Overlay Rules

Good:

- `Birthday gifts without guessing`
- `Baby shower wishlist made easy`
- `A wishlist next to your registry`
- `One list for family gifts`
- `Class gifts without the group chat`

Avoid:

- long sentences
- vague product claims
- too much brand explanation
- tiny script text that becomes hard to read on mobile

## Pinterest Asset Formula

Canvas:

- 1000x1500 or 1024x1536
- 2:3 vertical

Composition:

- realistic photo background
- overlay text in a clean readable font
- small brand line
- optional subtle translucent overlay behind text only if needed for contrast

CTA:

- link broad family/gift posts to `https://www.wishees.com/?lang=en`
- link creator/affiliate posts to `https://www.wishees.com/affiliate/program?lang=en`

## Video Asset Formula

Use the same photo-led world for YouTube Shorts and TikTok.

Use generated music or a light instrumental audio bed for each video so the asset feels active rather than like a static exported pin.

Structure:

1. First frame: realistic gift moment with short hook text.
2. Middle frames: 2 or 3 simple beats showing the gift problem.
3. End frame: one simple Wishees CTA.

Duration:

- 7 to 10 seconds for simple concept shorts.

Style:

- vertical 1080x1920
- slow zoom or gentle pan if using still photos
- generated light music/audio bed
- no busy transitions
- no tiny UI text
- no long explanatory paragraphs

Example birthday video sequence:

1. Photo: birthday cake and wrapped gifts.
   Text: `Birthday gifts without guessing`
2. Photo/detail: gift table.
   Text: `Share one simple list`
3. Wishees end card.
   Text: `Make a wishlist at Wishees.com`

## Decision Rule

Make this the default style for Wishees photos and videos unless:

- the asset needs to explain affiliate mechanics, or
- the asset is a product walkthrough, or
- a measured test shows graphic-style assets outperform photo-led assets for the same use case.
