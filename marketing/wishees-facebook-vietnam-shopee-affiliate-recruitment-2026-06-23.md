## Positioning

Wishees is not a replacement for Shopee Affiliate.

Wishees is a gift-list layer that helps creators and ordinary users turn product links into shareable birthday, baby shower, wedding, family, and holiday wishlists.

Correct economics:
- Creators can save their own Shopee affiliate tag where supported.
- When gift buyers shop through tagged links, Shopee or the relevant affiliate program decides attribution and commission under its own rules.
- Buyers pay the normal store price.
- Wishees does not pay creators directly and does not take the creator's affiliate commission.
- Do not promise guaranteed income.

Create one gift wishlist, add 3 Shopee items, share the link.
