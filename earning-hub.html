Operating rules
  • No prepayment, deposits, crypto, or card entry to unlock work.
  • Use PayPal where possible before sharing bank details.
  • Assessments, identity checks, and onboarding answers must be completed personally.
  • Affiliate links and sponsored placements must be clearly disclosed.

Affiliate Content Plan

Build trust first. Pages should compare tools honestly, mark paid links clearly, and avoid fake claims about income.

  • Publish a transparent disclosure block on any page that contains affiliate links.
  • Prioritize software and remote-work tools over physical products to avoid returns and product-safety issues.
  • Create comparison pages: paid user testing platforms, AI evaluator platforms, best payout trackers, best tools for remote testers.
  • Add one useful downloadable asset per article so the page has value even before affiliate approvals.
  • Track clicks and signups only with privacy-respecting analytics and cookie consent where required.

Hard Avoid List

Avoid any platform that asks for an upfront deposit, crypto transfer, card verification charge, paid training package, fake product purchase, fake review, bank login, or "unlock tasks" payment. Also avoid EU dropshipping until VAT, returns, product safety, packaging, guarantee, and customer-support obligations are deliberately handled.
This page is an earning experiment hub, not financial, tax, or legal advice.
