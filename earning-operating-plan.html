30-Day Remote Earning Operating Plan

A simple execution queue for building cashflow platforms, AI evaluation applications, downloadable assets, and future affiliate pages without upfront card spend.

Download plan Download content calendar

Week 1

Cashflow setup

  • Complete UserTesting practice test personally.
  • Create TestingTime and one crowdtesting profile.
  • Prepare device inventory and payout notes.

Week 2

AI evaluator pipeline

  • Apply to DataAnnotation.
  • Apply to TELUS Digital AI Community and Welocalize roles.
  • Practice concise rating explanations.

Week 3

Digital product shelf

  • Publish the three free lead magnets.
  • Prepare paid versions with examples and formats.
  • Choose one marketplace for payout setup.

Week 4

Affiliate and leads

  • Draft user testing and AI evaluator comparison pages.
  • Add disclosure before any paid links.
  • Create a QA review service path through contact.
Do not use automation to complete platform assessments, create fake reviews, bypass identity checks, or submit answers that are supposed to be personal. This plan is for preparation, publishing, tracking, and legitimate applications.
